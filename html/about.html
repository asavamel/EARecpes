This is a project of a programming student at Holmesglen TAFE at Chadstone. It is hoped that visitors to the site will contribute information regarding origins of the recipes, health attributes and historical events relating to some of the recipes

The purpose of the website is to give sample recipes of foods that are typical in the coastal regions of the East African country of KenyaÂ  and help individuals who seek diversity in the food they eat have a more geographically focused recipe set without having to or before travelling to these specific regions. The region represent a geographical blocks where several tribal groups are found speaking their native languages but can generally communicate in Swahili.

This website is targeted at middle income individuals, of any gender who fancy sampling traditional foods from Kenya and those who can share variations to these recipes. The target group would ideally have at least one hour to cook and have access to internet connection including the use of mobile devices. It is worth noting that Kenya and Tanzania share a wise coastline along the Indian Ocean and indeed share many of the ideas contained in these recipes.
